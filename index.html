Welcome to SGNS

Nurturing Character, Intellect, & Leadership

Shishu Gyan Nikunja is Jaleshwar's leading secondary boarding institution. We are firmly committed to building a learning culture centered on outstanding performance, paired with values of vision, integrity, and social responsibility.

Our boarding facility provides a safe, vibrant, and collaborative environment. By integrating a rigorous academic curriculum with outdoor learning, community engagement, and sports programs, we guide our students to realize their full potential and prepare them to tackle complex global challenges.

Academic Curriculums

We offer structured, progressive academic learning stages from Kindergarten check-ins up to Secondary Level Board Exams.

Kindergarten (KG - UKG)

Play-way and montessori inspired foundations. We focus on early literacy, numeracy, motor skills, social habits, and sensory exploration.

Explore Level ➜

Primary School (Grades 1 - 5)

Building core competencies in Language Arts, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, Creative Art, and basic computer check-ins.

Explore Level ➜

Secondary School (Grades 6 - 10)

Advanced studies in Science, Mathematics, English, Social Studies, and Computer Programming, preparing for SEE Board Examinations.
