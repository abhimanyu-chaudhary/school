About SGNS

About Shishu Gyan Nikunja

Established with a vision of creating a model educational institution in Nepal, Shishu Gyan Nikunja has been delivering high-quality, structured learning for over a decade. We are located in Jaleshwar, Nepal, offering comprehensive secondary education boarding facilities for students from kindergarten to grade 10.

Decade of Academic Excellence

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities including modern science laboratories, high-speed computer networks, a robust library system, and secure bus route tracking. Our highly qualified teaching staff works closely with students, providing a low teacher-to-student ratio of 1:15 to ensure personalized attention and optimal learning outcomes.

School Leadership & Administration

Our governing board and leadership team are composed of experienced educationalists, administrators, and social workers committed to guiding the institution with strategic direction and educational values.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Our Vision

To be a leading center of educational excellence in Nepal, cultivating visionary thinkers, critical problem solvers, and ethical leaders who are equipped to thrive in a globalized society.

Our Mission: Leading with Performance & Integrity

We commit to cultivating an academic culture centered on exceptional performance, paired with unwavering integrity. We guide our students to adapt, innovate, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Core CLEAR Values

  • Collaboration: Working together across teams, teachers, and parents for mutual growth.
  • Leadership: Taking ownership, taking initiative, and showing moral direction.
  • Excellence: Continually setting high academic standards and performance metrics.
  • Adaptability: Preparing for the future with computer coding and technology check-ins.
  • Respect: Valuing diversity, community engagement, and environmental stewardship.

Founder's Message

"Education is not merely the preparation for life; education is life itself. At Sagarmatha Gurukul, our dedication is to provide a holistic ecosystem where every child's talent is recognized, nurtured, and channeled to create a positive footprint in society."

In today's fast-changing digital age, students must not only memorize facts but also learn how to learn. Our board focuses on integrating state-of-the-art technological labs with traditional character values, offering an unbeatable foundation for secondary education check-ins. We welcome you to experience our campus and join our growing family.
