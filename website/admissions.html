Admission Procedures & Steps

We make our admission process straightforward and supportive. Follow these simple steps to enroll your child at Shishu Gyan Nikunja.

Step 1: Submit Application Form

Fill in the online registration form in the **Apply Now** tab, or visit the school office to get a paper brochure form.

Step 2: Admission Check-in & Written Test

Prospective students (from Grade 1 to 10) must appear for a written evaluation test checking skills in Mathematics, English, and Science.

Step 3: Interview Interaction

An interaction meeting is held with both the student and parent to discuss board facility expectations and academic pathways.

Step 4: Admission Approval & Fees Clearance

Once selected, parents must verify past academic reports, pay the admission fee at the cash office (or online UPI check), and secure the seat.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

At Sagarmatha Gurukul, we believe that monetary constraints should never block bright minds from accessing high-quality boarding education.

1. Academic Merit Scholarships

Awarded to students securing outstanding positions in standard school annual check-ins. Students with GPA 3.8+ in board exams get up to 50% tuition fee waivers.

2. Sports & Enrichment Aid

Offered to national-level or district-level sports medalists representing Rupandehi or Jaleshwar wards.

3. Need-Based Concessions

We offer financial help for students from low-income agricultural backgrounds or remote regions. Apply by submitting a community verification letter at the administration office.

💡

Interested candidates should submit the scholarship request letter along with their online application form under the **Apply Now** tab.
