Enrichment

Outdoor Learning & Leadership Camps

We believe that learning is most impactful when experienced in nature. Our outdoor curriculum instills self-reliance, teamwork, and ecological consciousness.

Himalayan Trekking & Nature Trails

Every year, students from Grades 7 to 10 participate in educational hikes and ecological mapping camps in the lower Himalayan foothills. Led by experienced certified guides and teachers, students learn navigation, outdoor shelter building, biodiversity mapping, and environmental sustainability practices.

Adventure Camps

Our annual leadership camps focus on survival skills, map reading, knot tying, and group challenge courses that build deep confidence, communication skills, and lifelong friendships.

Community Service & Social Outreach

Teaching our students to give back and serve the communities they belong to, fostering social responsibility and empathy.

Local Ward Development Campaigns

Sagarmatha Gurukul students participate in weekly community campaigns in Devdaha municipality. These include environmental cleanliness drives, tree plantation events, health hygiene check-in campaigns, and tutoring support for local government school kids.

Charity Drives & Relief Campaigns

During natural emergencies (like floods or cold waves), our Student Council organizes donation collection points, distributing blankets, non-perishable foodstuffs, and writing stationery to affected regions.

Athletics & Sports Programs

Encouraging healthy living, teamwork, and competitive spirit through daily physical fitness schedules and professional training check-ins.

⚽ Football & Volleyball

Our school has specialized outdoor grass fields for football and sand pits for volleyball, with professional coaches training the teams daily.

🏓 Table Tennis & Indoor Games

Equipped indoor sports rooms for table tennis tournaments, chess championships, and physical gymnastics classes.

Annual Sports Meet

Held in January, our Annual Sports Meet splits students into four school houses (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow) to compete in track athletics (100m, 400m, relay), high jump, shot put, and team sports, ending with a grand closing ceremony.
